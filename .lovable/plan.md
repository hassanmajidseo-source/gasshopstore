

# GasShop.store — Premium Gas Products Ecommerce Website

## Brand Identity
- **Colors**: Deep Blue (#1a3a5c), Orange (#f57c20), White — conveying trust, energy, and safety
- **Logo concept**: Flame + Shield icon representing safety and gas
- **Tagline options** (5):
  1. "Fueling Pakistan, Safely."
  2. "Your Trusted Gas Partner."
  3. "Safe Gas. Smart Choice."
  4. "Gas Solutions You Can Trust."
  5. "Powering Homes. Protecting Lives."
- **Tone**: Professional, confident, helpful

### 6. About Us

## Backend (Supabase / Lovable Cloud)

- **Products table**: name, description, price, category, images, specs, stock status
- **Orders table**: customer info, items, status, payment method (COD), delivery address
- **Categories table**: Home, Commercial, Industrial, Safety, etc.
- **Blog posts table**: for the safety education and blog content
- **Contact inquiries table**: form submissions
- **Admin panel**: Manage products, orders, blog posts, and inquiries

---

## Key Integrations

- **WhatsApp ordering**: Click-to-WhatsApp buttons with pre-filled order messages
- **Phone ordering**: Prominent phone number CTAs
- **COD flow**: Order placement with Cash on Delivery as primary method
